Newborn weight gain: What's normal and what's not

Your baby will more than likely lose weight in the first five to seven days. This is normal. In fact, a 5 percent weight loss is normal for formula-fed babies in the first week. Breastfed babies may lose 7 to 10 percent of their birth weight in the first week.
